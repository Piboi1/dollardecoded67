Coffee & Ingredients
$0.69

👨‍🍳
Labor & Benefits
$1.73
Wages for baristas, shift supervisors, and store managers, plus comprehensive benefits including health insurance, stock options, and the famous college tuition assistance program for partners (employees).
🏪
Store Operations
$0.92
Prime real estate costs, utilities, equipment maintenance, cleaning supplies, and everything needed to keep that cozy coffeehouse atmosphere running smoothly in premium locations.
📱
Corporate & Marketing
$1.15
Mobile app development, loyalty program rewards, marketing campaigns, new product development, supply chain management, and corporate headquarters operations that keep the Starbucks experience consistent globally.
🏛️
Taxes
$0.58
Federal, state, and local taxes including corporate income tax, property tax, sales tax, and payroll taxes. These contribute to public infrastructure, education, and community services.
📈
Shareholder Returns
$0.69
Returns to shareholders through dividends and stock appreciation. This includes individual investors, mutual funds, pension funds, and employee stock purchase plans, funding future expansion and innovation.

💡 Did You Know?

Starbucks serves over 100 million customers weekly across 33,000+ stores globally! Your $5.75 latte supports a massive network that includes coffee farmers, roasters, logistics teams, and baristas worldwide. Plus, Starbucks pays 100% of tuition for employees' bachelor's degrees!
